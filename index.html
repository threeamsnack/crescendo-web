About

Nestled within the bustling hub of Delhi University's cultural landscape, we bring to you the much-awaited annual fest of Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies, CRESCENDO. Over three lively days, witness the participation of eager students in this vibrant celebration, brimming with laughter and excitement

Starting with a dazzling Promenade, followed by two evenings of musical enchantment and thrilling activities. From street plays to dance performances, enter a world of melodic reverie. It's a time to reunite with friends, unwind, and simply bask in the joyous atmosphere

Last year we closed with a blast, and this year it’s back with a single promise: more fun and surprises. So, mark your calendars, register, and prepare to be swept away by the cosmic theme of "Space"
